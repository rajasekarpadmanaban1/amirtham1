About Us

Amirtham Renewables Pvt Ltd, formerly known as Amirtham Energy Enterprises, is a top-leading solar company based in Tamil Nadu, India. With overall 6+ years of experience in the solar industry, we have evolved from a startup to a private limited company, specializing in solar panel installation, manufacturing, and services.
